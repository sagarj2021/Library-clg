About our library :

Sinhgad Academy of Engineering (SAE) Library is most lively place in the college that enables learning & advancement of knowledge. The mission of SAE library is to disseminate knowledge & facilitate users for creation of new knowledge. SAE library is rich in collection which consist of Text books, Reference books, Encyclopedias, Dictionaries, Data books, E-books, Journals, Technical Magazines, General books, Books for competitive exams etc. It also subscribe E-journal packages of Engineering and Management disciplines of well known international publishers to enhance & promote research activity. Library also offers facilities like reading hall with the capacity of 300 students which provides conducive atmosphere for study, digital library for online journal access and internet searching, Web OPAC for easy access of library database, Reprography service, Book Bank etc. Library conducts extension services like book exhibition, orientation programs for new users and “Earn & Learn” scheme for needy students.
